Tour Overview

Day 1

Delhi - Manali

Pickup and transfer to Volvo bus stand. Depart for Manali at 5:00 PM. Overnight journey by Volvo bus.

Day 2

Manali Local Sightseeing

Arrive Manali, check into hotel. Visit Hadimba Temple, Club House, and Van Vihar. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3

Manali Snow Points

Visit George, Gulaba, Marhi, and Rohtang Pass (snow point, availability dependent). Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4

Manali - Kullu & Manikaran

Visit Kullu Valley, Kasol Valley, Angora Rabbit Farm, and Manikaran Temple. Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5

Manali - Delhi

Check out of hotel. Leisure time. Transfer to Volvo bus stand for 4:00 PM departure to Delhi. Overnight journey.

Day 6

Delhi Sightseeing

Arrive Delhi, check into hotel. Visit India Gate, Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Lotus Temple, and Qutub Minar. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 7

Agra - Delhi Departure

Visit Agra Fort and Taj Mahal. Return to Delhi and transfer to the airport for onward journey.
