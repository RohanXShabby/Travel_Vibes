Himachal Pradesh
The Land of Majestic Mountains

Himachal Pradesh, a breathtakingly beautiful state nestled in the northern Himalayas, is a haven for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those looking for a tranquil retreat. Known for its snow-covered peaks, lush valleys, and serene towns, Himachal is a treasure trove of beauty and culture. From the colonial charm of Shimla to the spiritual serenity of Dharamsala, and the enchanting landscapes of Manali and Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh offers something for everyone. Whether you're into adventure sports or prefer to unwind amidst nature, Himachal promises an unforgettable journey.

Himachal Tour

Tour Overview

Day 1

DELHI - SHIMLA

Arrive at Delhi, meet our representative, and proceed for a scenic drive to Shimla. On arrival, check into your hotel. In the evening, visit Mall Road and The Ridge. Overnight stay at the hotel in Shimla.

Day 2

SHIMLA - KUFRI - SHIMLA

After breakfast, explore Kufri, a famous hill station known for its wildlife zoo and scenic beauty. Visit Chail, Green Valley View, and return to Shimla for an evening of leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3

SHIMLA - MANALI

After breakfast, check out and proceed to Manali. Enjoy the picturesque Kullu Valley on the way, and reach Manali by evening. Check into your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4

MANALI - ROHTANG PASS

After breakfast, take a day trip to Rohtang Pass, enjoying breathtaking views and snow activities. Enroute, visit Rahala Waterfalls and Marhi. Return to Manali for an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5

MANALI - LOCAL SIGHTSEEING

Post-breakfast, explore Manali‚Äôs main attractions, including Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Kund, Solang Valley, and a Buddhist monastery. Return to the hotel in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day 6

MANALI - DHARAMSALA

After breakfast, proceed to Dharamsala. On arrival, check into your hotel and relax for the evening. Overnight stay at the hotel in Dharamsala.

Day 7

DHARAMSALA TOUR

Post-breakfast, visit Dal Lake, Bhagsu Nath Temple, Chamunda Devi Temple, and the Dalai Lama Palace. Return to your hotel in the evening for an overnight stay.

Day 8

DHARAMSALA - DALHOUSIE

After breakfast, depart for Dalhousie. Reach Dalhousie by late afternoon, check into the hotel, and enjoy some leisure time. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 9

DALHOUSIE TOUR

Spend the day exploring Khajjiar, Chamba, and Dalhousie. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 10

DALHOUSIE - DELHI

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival, you will be dropped at the airport with sweet memories of your Himachal tour.
