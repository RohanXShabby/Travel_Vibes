Shimla, Kullu, & Manali
The Ultimate Himachal Getaway

Shimla, Kullu, and Manali are three of the most stunning destinations in Himachal Pradesh, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural heritage. Shimla, known as the "Queen of Hills," is famous for its colonial charm and scenic views. Kullu, the "Valley of Gods," is a paradise for adventure seekers and spiritual travelers. Manali, nestled in the Beas River valley, is a perfect base for exploring snow-capped peaks, valleys, and adventure activities like skiing, trekking, and river rafting. Together, these destinations provide an unforgettable experience for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and those seeking peace and spirituality.

Tour Overview

Day 1

Delhi - Shimla

Arrive in Delhi and transfer to Shimla, a charming hill town in the Himalayas. Famous for colonial heritage, pine forests, and apple orchards, Shimla was once the British summer retreat. Check in at the hotel and relax for an overnight stay.

Day 2

Shimla & Kufri Excursion

After breakfast, visit Kufri‚Äôs lush landscapes, Himalayan Wildlife Zoo, and amusement park. Explore Shimla‚Äôs iconic spots, including Mall Road, Christ Church, and Jakhoo Temple. Enjoy shopping and caf√© hopping before returning to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3

Shimla - Manali

Depart for Manali after breakfast, enjoying scenic views of Beas-Sutlej Link, Pandoh Dam, and Kullu Valley. Stop for lunch en route (at own expense). Upon arrival, check in at the hotel, unwind, and enjoy a relaxing evening before an overnight stay.

Day 4

Rohtang Pass & Solang Valley

After breakfast, head to Rohtang Pass for breathtaking mountain views. Visit Rahala Waterfalls and Solang Valley for adventure activities. If Rohtang is closed, explore Snow Point instead. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5

Manali Sightseeing

Enjoy a half-day tour covering Hadimba Devi Temple, Vashisht Kund, Club House, and the Buddhist Monastery. Spend the evening at leisure before returning to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 6

Manali - Delhi Departure

After breakfast, drive back to Delhi. Arrive in the evening and transfer to the airport, concluding your trip with wonderful memories. Safe travels!
