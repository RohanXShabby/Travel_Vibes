Jaisalmer Desert
Golden Sands & Desert Adventures

The Jaisalmer Desert, also known as the Thar Desert, offers a mesmerizing landscape of golden sand dunes and a rich cultural experience. Jaisalmer, the "Golden City," serves as the gateway to this enchanting desert, where you can embark on unforgettable camel safaris, witness breathtaking sunsets, and immerse yourself in the unique desert lifestyle.

Tour Overview

Day 1

Arrival at Jaipur and Local Sightseeing

Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our friendly executives and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, embark on a full-day sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Visit the Albert Hall Museum, explore the intricate designs of Hawa Mahal, and immerse yourself in the vibrant local markets. In the evening, return to your hotel for an overnight stay at Wall Street Hotel.

Day 2

Jaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, start your full-day local sightseeing tour of Jaipur. Visit Statue Circle, Birla Mandir, Jantar Mantar, and City Palace. In the evening, enjoy free time for shopping. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay at Wall Street Hotel.

Day 3

Transfer to Bikaner via Ajmer & Pushkar

After breakfast, check out and head to Bikaner. Enroute, visit Ajmer Dargah and Ana Sagar Lake. Continue to Pushkar and visit the Brahma Temple and the sacred ghats. Afterward, proceed to Bikaner and check-in at Raj Haveli for an overnight stay.

Day 4

Transfer to Jaisalmer and Bikaner Sightseeing

After breakfast, check out and explore Bikaner. Visit the Junagarh Fort, Karni Mata Temple, and Camel Breeding Farm. Then, proceed to Jaisalmer and check in at Deoki Niwas Hotel. Relax and enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

Day 5

Jaisalmer City and Desert Sightseeing

Explore the Jaisalmer Fort and ancient havelis like Patwon Ki Haveli and Nathmal Ki Haveli. Visit Gadi Sagar Lake before heading to the Jaisalmer Desert for a camel safari. Enjoy cultural performances, a bonfire, and a traditional dinner at Bhati Desert Camp.

Day 6

Transfer to Jodhpur via War Museum & Mini Dubai

After breakfast, check out and head to Jodhpur. Enroute, stop at the War Museum and Mini Dubai. Continue your journey and check in at Kuchaman Haveli for a relaxing overnight stay.

Day 7

Jodhpur Sightseeing & Transfer to Udaipur

Visit the stunning Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and Umaid Bhawan Palace. After sightseeing, proceed towards Udaipur and check in at Radisson Green Hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 8

Udaipur Sightseeing

Explore the beauty of Udaipur with visits to the City Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake, Saheliyon ki Bari, and other famous landmarks. Enjoy the local charm and return to your hotel for an overnight stay at Radisson Green Hotel.

Day 9

Udaipur to Jaipur Drop

After breakfast, check out and head to Udaipur Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, concluding your Rajasthan tour.
