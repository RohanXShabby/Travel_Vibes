Jaipur
The Pink City: Royal Grandeur & Vibrant Culture

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is a city steeped in royal history and vibrant culture. Known as the "Pink City" for its distinctive rose-colored buildings, Jaipur offers a captivating blend of ancient heritage and modern charm. From the majestic forts and palaces to the bustling markets and colorful festivals, Jaipur provides a unique and unforgettable experience.

Jaipur Tour

Tour Overview

Day 1

Arrival in Jaipur and Sightseeing

Upon arrival in Jaipur, you will be greeted by the agent's representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, visit the Birla Temple, one of the largest in Jaipur. Then, head to Nahargarh Fort for breathtaking views of the city, especially at sunset. End the day with a visit to Chokhi Dhani, where you can enjoy camel rides, live performances, and authentic Rajasthani cuisine. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2

Jaipur City Tour

After breakfast, embark on a full day of sightseeing. Visit the City Palace, showcasing a fusion of Mughal and Rajput architecture, followed by the Hawa Mahal, also known as the "Palace of Winds." Explore the Jantar Mantar observatory, Jal Mahal in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, and the Albert Hall Museum, a popular attraction with splendid architecture. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 3

Drop at Jaipur Airport / Railway Station

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed to Jaipur Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, marking the end of your 2Nights/3Days Rajasthan tour.
