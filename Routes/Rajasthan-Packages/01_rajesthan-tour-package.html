Tour Overview

Day 1

Arrival in Jaipur

Pick up from Jaipur airport and drive to the hotel. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. Later, visit Amer Fort (magnificent fort with an extensive palace complex made of yellow and pink sandstone and white marble), Nahargarh Fort (situated on the Aravalli Ranges, offering a spectacular view of Jaipur), and Jaigarh Fort (located 500 feet above sea level, housing the world's largest cannon, 'Jaivana Cannon'). Evening free for visiting the local market. Night stay at Jaipur.

Day 2

Jaipur City Tour

After breakfast, visit City Palace (reflects the fusion of Mughal and Rajput architecture), Hawa Mahal (also known as the "Palace of Winds"), Jantar Mantar (one of the five observatories, the largest of which is in Jaipur), Jal Mahal (Water Palace in the middle of Man Sagar Lake), and Albert Hall Museum (a popular museum in Jaipur). Night stay at Jaipur.

Day 3

Jaipur to Bikaner

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Bikaner. Enroute, visit Karni Mata Temple (also known as the White Rat Temple). Check-in at the hotel in Bikaner. After freshening up, visit the Camel Breeding Farm (National Research Center and Asiaâ€™s largest farm). Night stay at Bikaner.

Day 4

Bikaner Sightseeing & Drive to Jaisalmer

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit Junagarh Fort and Lallgarh Fort (includes Anup Mahal, Gaj Mandir, Sheesh Mahal, Prachina Museum, and Havelis). After lunch, drive to Jaisalmer. Night stay at Jaisalmer.

Day 5

Jaisalmer Sightseeing with Sam Dunes

After breakfast, visit Patwon Ki Haveli, Salim Singh Ki Haveli, Nathmal Ki Haveli, Jaisalmer Fort, and Gadisar Lake. After lunch, drive to Sam Dunes. Check-in at the tent and freshen up. Enjoy a Camel Ride, a Cultural Program, and a Dinner at the dunes. Night stay at Sam Dunes.

Day 6

Jaisalmer to Jodhpur

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Jodhpur. Check-in at the hotel. After freshening up, visit Mehrangarh Fort (perched 100 feet above the city, made from red sandstone), Jaswant Thada (a cenotaph made from intricately carved marble), and Umaid Bhawan Palace. Night stay at Jodhpur.

Day 7

Jodhpur to Mount Abu

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Mount Abu. Visit Nakki Lake, Sunset Point, and Dilwara Jain Temples. Night stay at Mount Abu.

Day 8

Mount Abu to Udaipur

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Udaipur. Check-in at the hotel. Later, visit Jagdish Temple, Ropeway, and Sukhadiya Circle. Night stay at Udaipur.

Day 9

Udaipur Sightseeing

After breakfast, spend the day visiting City Palace, Lake Pichola, Fateh Sagar Lake, Pratap Memorial, and Saheliyon Ki Bari. Night stay at Udaipur.

Day 10

Departure from Udaipur

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and head to Jaipur Airport / Railway Station for your onward journey.
