Mussoorie, Corbett & Nainital
Colonial Charm, Wildlife Thrills & Serene Lakes

Mussoorie, Corbett, and Nainital offer a perfect blend of scenic beauty, wildlife adventure, and peaceful retreats. Mussoorie, the "Queen of Hills," captivates visitors with its colonial charm, bustling Mall Road, and breathtaking views of the Himalayas. Corbett National Park, India's oldest national park, is a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts, offering thrilling safaris and a chance to spot the majestic Bengal tiger. Nainital, the "Lake District of India," is known for its serene lakes, lush hills, and vibrant local markets, making it an ideal getaway for nature lovers. This diverse itinerary ensures a memorable experience, from tranquil boat rides to thrilling jungle safaris.
