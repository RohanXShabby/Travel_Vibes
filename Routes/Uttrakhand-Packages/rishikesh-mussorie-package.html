Rishikesh & Mussoorie
Spiritual Bliss & Colonial Charm

Rishikesh and Mussoorie offer a perfect blend of spirituality, adventure, and scenic beauty. Rishikesh, the Yoga Capital of the World, is famous for its sacred Ganga Aarti, ancient temples, serene ashrams, and thrilling river rafting experiences. Mussoorie, often called the "Queen of Hills," enchants visitors with its colonial-era architecture, bustling Mall Road, and breathtaking views of the Doon Valley. Whether you seek spiritual retreats or tranquil mountain escapes, this journey promises a rejuvenating experience.
