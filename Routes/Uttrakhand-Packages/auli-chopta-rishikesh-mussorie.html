Rishikesh, Auli, Chopta & Mussoorie
Spiritual Bliss, Snowy Slopes, Untamed Wilderness & Colonial Charm

Rishikesh, Auli, Chopta, and Mussoorie offer a perfect blend of spirituality, adventure, and natural beauty. Rishikesh, the Yoga Capital of the World, is known for its temples, ashrams, and thrilling river rafting. Auli, a paradise for skiing enthusiasts, boasts mesmerizing snow-covered peaks. Chopta, the "Mini Switzerland of India," is a haven for trekkers and nature lovers. Mussoorie, the "Queen of Hills," captivates visitors with its colonial charm and panoramic views. This diverse itinerary promises an unforgettable experience, from spiritual retreats to adventure-packed escapades.

It literally means "Moon Rock". It is located at a height of about 4000 metres above sea level. This peak provides views of the Himalayas, including Nandadevi, Trisul, Kedar Peak, Bandarpunch and Chaukhamba peaks. There are various legends associated with this placeDinner & night stay at Camps in Chopta.
