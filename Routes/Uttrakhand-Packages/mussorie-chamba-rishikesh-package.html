Mussoorie, Chamba & Rishikesh
Colonial Charm, Himalayan Serenity & Spiritual Bliss

Mussoorie, Chamba, and Rishikesh offer a perfect mix of scenic beauty, peaceful retreats, and spiritual experiences. Mussoorie, the "Queen of Hills," enchants visitors with its colonial charm, bustling Mall Road, and panoramic views. Chamba, a serene hill station, offers breathtaking Himalayan vistas, lush greenery, and a peaceful escape from city life. Rishikesh, the "Yoga Capital of the World," is known for its sacred Ganga Aarti, ancient temples, yoga retreats, and thrilling river rafting. This itinerary blends adventure, spirituality, and relaxation, promising an unforgettable journey.
