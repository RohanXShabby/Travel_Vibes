Ladakh
Land of High Passes

Ladakh, often called the "Land of High Passes," is a union territory in the northernmost part of India. Known for its dramatic landscapes, barren mountains, high-altitude deserts, pristine lakes, and unique culture, Ladakh offers an unparalleled travel experience. This region is a haven for adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and those seeking a glimpse into a distinct way of life. From trekking across challenging terrains to exploring ancient monasteries, Ladakh's rugged beauty and serene atmosphere leave a lasting impression on every visitor.

Tour Overview

Day 01

Arrive Leh

Upon arrival in Leh, meet and receive assistance at the airport before transferring to the hotel. Spend the day at leisure for acclimatization to the high altitude. Enjoy meals and overnight stay at Hotel Royal Heritage or similar.

Day 02

In Leh | Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, visit Sankar Gompa, Shanti Stupa, and Leh Palace. Explore Stok Palace, Sindhu Darshan, Kali Mandir, Hall of Fame, Magnetic Hill, and Pather Sahib. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure. Meals and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 03

In Leh | Monastery Tour

After an early breakfast, visit Shey Palace, Thicksey Monastery, and Hemis Monastery, known for its summer festivals. Explore the 36-ft-high Buddha statue at Shey and the multi-storeyed Thicksey Monastery. Return to Leh in the evening for meals and an overnight stay.

Day 04

In Leh | Excursion to Khardung La

After breakfast, drive to Khardung La, the world's highest motorable road at 18,380 ft, serving as a gateway to the Siachen Glacier. Enjoy the scenic beauty before returning to Leh. Evening visit to a Ladakhi family. Meals and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 05

Leh Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Leh Airport for your onward flight. Depart with unforgettable memories of your Ladakh journey.
