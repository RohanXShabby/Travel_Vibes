Mata Vaishno Devi
Abode of the Divine

Mata Vaishno Devi, nestled in the Trikuta Mountains of Jammu and Kashmir, India, is a revered Hindu pilgrimage site. The holy shrine, located within a cave, is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Vaishnavi, an incarnation of Durga. Millions of devotees visit the shrine each year, trekking the challenging path to seek blessings and spiritual solace. The journey to the shrine is considered a sacred pilgrimage, filled with faith and devotion. Mata Vaishno Devi is more than just a place of worship; it's an experience that embodies the power of belief and the enduring strength of faith.

Tour Overview

Day 01

Jammu - Katra (48 kms / approx 2 hour drive)

Arrive in Jammu and transfer to Katra by air-conditioned car. Enjoy a Hovercraft ride on River Tawi en route. Check-in at the hotel and relax for the day, exploring local surroundings at leisure.

Day 02

Vaishno Devi Darshan & Return

Early morning, take a helicopter ride to Vaishno Devi. Walk 2 km from Sanjhichat for Darshan. Return to Katra, then proceed to Jammu Railway Station for departure with memorable experiences.
