Kashmir
The Paradise on Earth

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is a region nestled in the Himalayas, renowned for its breathtaking beauty. With its snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, pristine lakes, and vibrant culture, Kashmir offers a diverse range of experiences. From serene shikara rides on Dal Lake to thrilling treks in the mountains, Kashmir captivates the senses and leaves a lasting impression on every visitor. The region's rich history, unique cuisine, and warm hospitality further enhance its charm. Kashmir is a destination that truly lives up to its name, offering a glimpse of heaven on earth.

Tour Overview

Day 01

Arrive Srinagar | Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Welcome to Srinagar, the Lake City. On arrival at Srinagar Airport, begin your tour of 'Crown of Kashmir'. Transfer to a Houseboat and proceed on a city tour visiting Shankaracharya Temple, Mughal Gardens, and local markets. Visit a handicraft shop for authentic Kashmiri items. Enjoy a leisurely stroll along Dal Lake in the evening. Overnight stay at the Houseboat.

Day 02

In Srinagar | Day Excursion to Sonamarg

Embark on a full-day excursion to Sonamarg, the 'Meadow of Gold'. The Sindh River makes it a popular spot for trout and mahseer fishing. Opt for a pony ride to the glacier for fun in the snow (at your own expense). Return to Srinagar in the evening for an overnight stay at the Houseboat.

Day 03

On to Pahalgam | Srinagar - Pahalgam

Early morning departure for Pahalgam, the 'Valley of Shepherds'. En route, visit Awantipura Ruins, an 1100-year-old temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Enjoy the scenic beauty of Pahalgam or take a short walk to Baisaran. Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.

Day 04

On to Gulmarg | Pahalgam - Gulmarg

After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg, the 'Meadow of Flowers'. Enjoy the picturesque route and European-style hill station ambiance. Explore the 18-hole Golf Course, the highest in the world. Spend the evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Gulmarg.

Day 05

On to Srinagar | Gulmarg - Srinagar

After breakfast, enjoy the Gondola Cable Car Ride, the highest in Asia (at your own cost). Engage in photography, horse riding, and shopping before returning to Srinagar in the evening. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.

Day 06

In Srinagar | Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Today after breakfast, visit the famous Mughal Gardens in Srinagar, including Nishat Bagh (the Garden of Pleasure) and Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love). These gardens are located along the banks of Dal Lake and follow a symmetrical layout with central waterways and fountains. Also, visit the Shankaracharya Temple for a panoramic view of Srinagar. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 07

Return Home with Happy Memories

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport to board your flight with unforgettable memories of your Kashmir journey.
