Port Blair
Spiritual Bliss & Majestic Beauty

Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is a charming destination that blends rich history, natural beauty, and a unique cultural heritage. Located on the picturesque Bay of Bengal, Port Blair offers visitors a serene escape with pristine beaches, historical landmarks, and an unforgettable tropical experience. This coastal town is the gateway to explore the Andaman Islands, offering both relaxation and adventure.

Tour Overview

Day 01

Arrival Port Blair + Half Day Local Sightseeing

Arrive at Port Blair, meet our escort at the airport, and check-in to your hotel. After lunch and refreshment, visit the historic Cellular Jail followed by the Light & Sound Show, narrating its history. Return to the hotel for the night.

Day 02

Excursion to North Bay & Ross Island

After breakfast, embark on a Sea Safari to North Bay Coral Island and Ross Island. Experience cruising and water activities with expert assistance. Return to Port Blair and transfer to the hotel.

Day 03

Excursion to Havelock Island

After breakfast, take a ferry to Havelock Island (approx. 2.5 hours). Visit Radhanagar Beach, ranked 7th among Asia‚Äôs top beaches. After lunch, return to Port Blair by ferry.

Day 04

Departure from Port Blair

Enjoy a relaxing morning with breakfast. Free day for leisure. In the evening, enjoy a tasty dinner on a Dhow Cruise. Return to the hotel for the night.

Day 05

Departure

Morning free for packing and personal activities. Check out by 08:00 AM and transfer to the airport. Tour ends.
