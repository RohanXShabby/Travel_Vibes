Tour Overview

Day 01

Arrival Port Blair + Half Day Local Sightseeing

You arrive in Port Blair, where our escorts receive you at the airport. After check-in, lunch, and refreshment, visit the historic Cellular Jail, followed by the Light & Sound Show, which narrates its history. (Local sightseeing points are closed on Monday & Govt. holidays.) Return to the hotel for the night.

Day 02

Excursion to North Bay & Ross Island

After breakfast, embark on a Sea Safari to North Bay Coral Island & Ross Island. Enjoy cruising and water activities with onboard crew assistance. Upon return to Port Blair, our escorts will arrange transfers back to the hotel. (Carry changing clothes, water & dry food.)

Day 03

Port Blair to Havelock Island

Early departure for Havelock Island, cruise at 08:30 AM (report 1 hour early). The journey takes about 1.5 hours. Upon arrival, meet our escort, check in, and post-lunch visit Radhanagar Beach for sunset. (Boat transfers are subject to availability and weather conditions.)

Day 04

Havelock Island to Neil Island

Check out from the resort in the morning (around 9:00 AM) and proceed to board the cruise to Neil Island. Upon arrival, meet our escorts, check in at the hotel, and post-lunch visit the beaches around the island. Overnight stay in Neil Island.

Day 05

Neil Island to Port Blair

After breakfast, visit Sitapur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach, and Bharatpur Beach, ideal for swimming and relaxation. Later, transfer to Neil Jetty for the return ferry to Port Blair. Upon arrival, transfer to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 06

Excursion to Baratang Island & Back

Early morning departure (around 3:30 AM) for Baratang Island (110 km drive). The journey passes through dense forests and includes ferry crossings. Visit the mesmerizing Limestone Caves via a scenic mangrove creek journey by boat. Return to Port Blair by evening. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 07

Port Blair Full Day Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely Port Blair City Tour, including Chatham Saw Mill (Asia's oldest and largest), the Anthropological Museum, Samudrika Marine Museum, and shopping at Sagarika, a government-run handicrafts emporium. Post lunch, visit Corbyn‚Äôs Cove Beach, a coconut palm-fringed beach ideal for swimming and sunbathing. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 08

Departure from Port Blair

Morning free for packing and personal activities. Check out by 08:00 AM and transfer to the airport for departure. Tour ends.
