Tour Overview

Day 1

Kochi Sightseeing Tour (40kms-1hrs)

Welcome to God's Own Country! Upon arrival at Cochin Airport, our representative will greet you and transfer you to the hotel. After check-in, embark on a half-day sightseeing tour of Kochi. Explore St. Francis Church, the iconic Chinese fishing nets, Santa Cruz Basilica, Jewish Synagogue, Jewish Street, and Mattancherry Palace (Dutch Palace). Overnight stay in Kochi.

Day 2

Journey to Munnar (150Kms-4hrs)

After a delicious breakfast, proceed to Munnar, a scenic hill station known for its misty mountains, sprawling tea gardens, and wildlife sanctuaries. En route, admire the breathtaking waterfalls of Cheeyappara & Valara. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Spend the rest of the day exploring the serene natural surroundings. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3

Exploring Munnar

After breakfast, visit Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Continue to Mattupetty & Kundala Dam, where you can enjoy a boat ride surrounded by tea gardens and lush meadows. Visit Echo Point, famous for its natural echo phenomenon, and explore Shooting Point and the Tea Museum. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 4

Discovering Thekkady (140km-4hrs)

After breakfast, check out and drive to Thekkady, known for its spice plantations and diverse wildlife. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit Periyar National Park and enjoy a wildlife cruise on Periyar Lake. Engage in activities like spice plantation tours, bamboo rafting, jungle trekking, and elephant rides. Don't forget to explore the local spice market for fresh produce and handicrafts. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 5

Alleppey - The Venice of the East (180km-5hrs)

After breakfast, head to Alleppey, a tranquil coastal city famous for its backwaters. Enjoy a houseboat ride through the coconut-fringed canals or spend your time unwinding by the serene lagoons and beaches. Take in the mesmerizing sunset and explore the local culture. Overnight stay in Alleppey.

Day 6

Departure from Alleppey to Cochin (120km-3hrs)

After breakfast, pack your bags with beautiful memories of Kerala. Proceed to Cochin Airport on time for your return journey. Wishing you a pleasant trip back home. See you again in Kerala!
