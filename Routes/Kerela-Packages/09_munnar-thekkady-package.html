Munnar
Serenity, Scenic Beauty & Timeless Traditions

Munnar , nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, is a breathtaking hill station renowned for its sprawling tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, and lush greenery. This tranquil haven offers a perfect escape for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat. From exploring verdant tea estates to trekking through misty hills and witnessing cascading waterfalls, Munnar provides an unforgettable experience.

Welcome to God's Own Country! Upon arrival, our representative will greet you at Cochin Airport and proceed to Munnar. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and unwind in the comforts of your room. Spend the rest of the day exploring the natural beauty around you. Overnight stay in Munnar.
