Tour Overview

Day 1

Kochi Sightseeing Tour (40kms-1hr)

Welcome to God's Own Country! Upon arrival at Cochin Airport/Railway Station, our representative will greet you and transfer you to the hotel. Check-in and relax before heading out for a half-day sightseeing tour of Kochi. Visit major attractions like St. Francis Church, Chinese fishing nets, Santa Cruz Basilica, Jewish Synagogue, Jewish Street, and Mattancherry Palace/Dutch Palace. Overnight stay in Kochi. (Note: Dutch Palace & Jewish Synagogue are closed on Fridays & Saturdays.)

Day 2

Munnar Hill Station Tour (150kms-4hrs)

After breakfast, proceed to Munnar, a picturesque hill station known for its rolling tea plantations, mist-covered mountains, and scenic beauty. En route, witness the stunning Cheeyappara & Valara waterfalls. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and unwind amidst nature. Spend the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Munnar Sightseeing Tour

Stop by Echo Point, Shooting Point, and the Tea Museum to learn about Munnarâ€™s tea heritage. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 4

Thekkady Tour Package (140kms-4hrs)

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady, a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Check in at the hotel and relax. Later, visit Periyar National Park for a thrilling wildlife cruise on Periyar Lake. Day 5

Thekkady to Gavi Sightseeing (40kms-1hr)

Early morning, head to Gavi, an eco-tourism hotspot known for its pristine landscapes. After breakfast, explore the plantations with a guided tour, followed by a rowboat ride and a visit to a cardamom plantation. Enjoy panoramic views of the Sabarimala mountains and visit the wildlife museum. Return to Thekkady in the evening. (Note: Off-road vehicle & Gavi package costs are extra.) Overnight stay in Thekkady.

Day 6

Alleppey Backwater Cruise (140kms-4hrs)

After breakfast, proceed to Alleppey, the Venice of the East. Check in to a traditional houseboat and enjoy a cruise through the scenic backwaters lined with coconut groves. Relax and witness the unique village life of Kerala. Overnight stay in the houseboat.

Day 7

Kanyakumari Tour Package (280kms-7hrs)

After breakfast, check out and drive to Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India. Visit attractions like Suchithram Temple, Devi Kanyakumari Temple, Vivekananda Rock Memorial, and Triveni Sangam. Witness a mesmerizing sunset over the confluence of three seas. Overnight stay in Kanyakumari.

Day 8

Kovalam Beach Tour (180kms-5hrs)

After breakfast, proceed to Kovalam, a world-famous beach destination. Check in at the resort and spend the rest of the day relaxing on the palm-fringed beach. The rocky promontory ensures safe swimming and water sports. Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Overnight stay in Kovalam.

Day 10

Kovalam to Trivandrum Departure (40kms-1hr)

Enjoy breakfast before checking out. Bid farewell to Kerala with wonderful memories. Proceed to Trivandrum Airport for your onward journey. Have a safe trip home!
