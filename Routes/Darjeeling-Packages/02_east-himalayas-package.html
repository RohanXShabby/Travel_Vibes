East Himalayas | Gangtok
Hills, Culture & Tranquility

Gangtok, the capital city of Sikkim, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, is a charming hill station offering a blend of natural beauty, vibrant culture, and serene tranquility. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and meandering rivers, Gangtok provides a picturesque escape. Explore ancient monasteries, indulge in local cuisine, and experience the unique blend of tradition and modernity. Gangtok is the perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat amidst breathtaking landscapes.

Day 01

Arrival NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport ‚Äì Gangtok

On arrival at NJP Railway Station / IXB Airport, transfer to Gangtok (5,500 ft.). Check-in to your hotel and enjoy the rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 02

Gangtok Local Sightseeing

Embark on a full-day city tour, visiting Tashi Viewpoint, Ganesh Tok, Hanuman Tok, Flower Show, Cottage Industry and Handicraft Centre, Institute of Tibetology, Dro-dul Chorten, Enchey Monastery, and Banjakhri Falls (maximum 6 hours). Overnight stay at Gangtok.

Day 03

Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir

After breakfast, embark on an excursion to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft.) and Baba Mandir (13,200 ft.), located 55 kms one way from Gangtok city. Overnight stay at Gangtok. (In case Tsomgo Lake is closed due to landslides or other reasons, alternate sightseeing will be provided.)
