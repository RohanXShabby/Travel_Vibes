Badrinath
Spiritual Bliss & Majestic Himalayas

Badrinath, one of the four sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites, is nestled in the majestic Himalayas along the banks of the Alaknanda River. Home to the revered Badrinath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu, this holy town is a perfect blend of spiritual significance and breathtaking natural beauty. Surrounded by snow-clad peaks, serene landscapes, and ancient legends, Badrinath attracts pilgrims and travelers seeking peace and devotion. From sacred rituals at the temple to exploring nearby attractions like Mana Village, this journey offers a divine and unforgettable experience.
