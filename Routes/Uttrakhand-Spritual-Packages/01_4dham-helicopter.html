Chardham Yatra

Tour Overview

Day 1

Dehradun to Yamunotri

Your Yamunotri yatra begins with a helicopter ride from Dehradun's Sahastradhara helipad to Kharsali. After arriving, you'll be transferred to your hotel and enjoy breakfast. A 5km palki ride awaits, taking you through scenic landscapes for VIP darshan at the Yamunotri temple. Depart your hotel early for this spiritual journey.

Day 2

Yamunotri to Gangotri

Explore Uttarakhand's "mini Switzerland," Gangotri. After arriving and checking into a luxurious hotel, enjoy an early breakfast before a 20km cab ride for VIP darshan at Gangotri temple. Enjoy the scenic beauty and premium amenities including ensuite rooms, vegetarian meals, and local sightseeing. Day two ends with dinner and a bonfire. An early breakfast prepares you for the journey to Kedarnath the following day.

Day 3

Gangotri to Kedarnath

Experience the spiritual heart of the Chardham yatra at Kedarnath. Fly from Gangotri to Guptkashi/Phata, then take a short 10-minute helicopter ride to Kedarnath (delays possible). Enjoy the breathtaking views upon arrival. With VIP darshan and Jal Abhishek arranged, immerse yourself in the temple's sanctity. Overnight stay is at Guptkashi after returning from Kedarnath helipad. Conclude the day with a delicious dinner and restful sleep.

Day 4

Kedarnath to Badrinath

Day four of your Chardham yatra takes you to Badrinath, offering stunning Himalayan views. After arriving at the Badrinath helipad, you'll be transferred to your hotel for breakfast/lunch and VIP darshan at the Badrinarayan temple. Enjoy leisure time exploring the valley near the sacred site. The day concludes with a comfortable stay and dinner at your hotel, allowing you to fully appreciate the Valley of Lord Narayan.

Day 5

Badrinath to Dehradun

In the morning after having a hearty breakfast, bow your head to Badri Narayan temple and fly back to Dehradun with positive vibes and pleasant memories of Chardham Yatra by helicopter package.
