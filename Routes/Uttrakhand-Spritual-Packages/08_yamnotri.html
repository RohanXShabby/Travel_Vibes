Yamunotri
Source of the Sacred Yamuna River

Yamunotri, the sacred source of the Yamuna River, is a revered pilgrimage site in the Garhwal Himalayas. Surrounded by snow-clad peaks and lush valleys, it is the first stop of the Char Dham Yatra. The Yamunotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Yamuna, is a place of deep spiritual significance, attracting devotees seeking blessings and purification. From the holy dip at Surya Kund to the scenic trek through rugged terrains, this journey offers a blend of devotion and adventure.
