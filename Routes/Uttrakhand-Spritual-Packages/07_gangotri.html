Gangotri
Spiritual Tranquility & Himalayan Majesty

Gangotri, one of the revered Char Dham pilgrimage sites, is nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas along the banks of the sacred Bhagirathi River. Home to the famous Gangotri Temple, dedicated to Goddess Ganga, this spiritual destination is where the holy river is believed to have descended to Earth. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and breathtaking landscapes, Gangotri attracts pilgrims and nature lovers seeking peace and devotion. From sacred rituals at the temple to exploring the serene beauty of the Himalayas, this journey offers a divine and unforgettable experience.

Continue the journey to Uttarkashi, a spiritual town on the banks of the Bhagirathi River. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and rest overnight.

Uttarkashi ‚Äì Gangotri ‚Äì Uttarkashi

After breakfast, drive to Gangotri, the origin of the sacred Ganges River. En route, witness picturesque landscapes and the stunning Bhagirathi peaks. Upon reaching Gangotri, take a holy dip in the Bhagirathi River and offer prayers at the Gangotri Temple. Visit sacred sites like Bhagirath Shila and Pandava Gufa. Return to Uttarkashi by evening for an overnight stay.

Uttarkashi ‚Äì Rishikesh ‚Äì Haridwar
